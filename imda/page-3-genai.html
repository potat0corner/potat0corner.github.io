What is Generative AI?

Generative AI is a type of AI that specialises in creating new content rather than just processing existing information.

Creation

Generation of new content: images, text, music, video.

Adaptation

Improving their output based on data to fit users' needs.

Automation

Automation of content creation processes, saving time and effort in fields like design.

Generation of unique images from simple descriptions or prompts.
