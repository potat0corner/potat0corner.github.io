What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the science of teaching machines to mimic human behaviours.
AI systems are taught and trained using computer programming that learns and adapts.

Learning

AI systems learn from data, improving over time through pattern recognition.

Reasoning

AI analyses data to make decisions using logic and probability.

Problem Solving

AI solves complex problems, providing solutions for challenges like diagnostics and logistics.

Creating

AI generates artwork, text, and even music based on user input.
