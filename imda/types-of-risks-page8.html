Types of AI-generated Risks

Images

Editing pictures to change appearances, widely used on social media.

Video deepfake

Improving their output based on data to fit users' needs.

Audio deepfake

Creating voice recordings that mimic someone's voice, used for deception.

Real-time / Live deepfake

Generating instant audio and video copies, used for immersive experiences or controlling others.

Text generation

Creating fake text imitating someone's writing style, used for spreading misinformation.
